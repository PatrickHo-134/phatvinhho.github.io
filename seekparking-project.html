Parking Hunt - Mobile application

A major problem of occasional car drivers in Melbourne CBD is to locate vacant car parking spaces. They usually feel frustrated and spend a minimum of 20 to 30 minutes circling around a destination to look for a vacant car park. This leads to excess traffic congestion and emission.

SeekParking is created to help overcome this problem. It is an Android mobile application which helps users reduce the time spent looking for parking spaces to 10 to 15 minutes. The App consists of important features including searching for available parking spaces close by a destination, navigating to an exact parking location, filtering of parking spaces based on parking time limits, setting alert for parking completion time, finding their car after they parked and finding possible available vacant car park at their arrival time. Each of these features is explained in the following sections. The app can be downloaded from Google Play. User may need to register on Google Play for downloading the application.

Note: Due to a copyright issue related to the name ParkingHunt, we have changed the name of the app to SeekParking since version 2. Sorry if it causes you any confusion.
